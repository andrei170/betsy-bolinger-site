02 — The four lanes she owns

Real substance in the deals that reward experience.

VA & Military LoansThe corridor specialty

Central Texas is one of the highest-volume VA-loan markets in the country, and VA financing has rules that quietly kill deals - occupancy timelines, appraisal minimum property requirements, funding-fee handling. Betsy has run these for two decades.

  • PCS timing and long-distance offers handled before you land
  • VA appraisal and MPR issues spotted before they cost you
  • $0-down entitlement structured right the first time

New ConstructionTo-be-built & specialty homes

Around Austin, new construction is a huge share of what is for sale - and the builder's sales rep works for the builder, not for you. Betsy reads the contract, times the inspections, and protects your earnest money and your upgrades.

  • Builder-contract review so you know what you signed
  • Upgrade and lot-premium decisions that hold their value
  • Third-party and final walk inspections, timed right

First-Time BuyersPatient, plain-language guidance

The biggest purchase of your life should not feel like a language you do not speak. Betsy walks first-timers through every step, in English or Spanish, so you sign knowing exactly what you agreed to and why.

  • Down-payment and closing-cost options explained plainly
  • Texas down-payment-assistance programs, where you qualify
  • Every contingency and deadline walked through, no jargon

Relocation & ResaleIn, out, and listing

Moving to Central Texas sight-unseen, leaving it for the next assignment, or selling the home you already own - Betsy handles the logistics and prices the listing on what the market is actually doing, not on a guess.

  • Remote and out-of-state buyers guided end to end
  • Resale listings priced and prepped for the current market
  • Neighborhood, school, and commute guidance for newcomers
03 — What working with a veteran looks like

Four steps. No pressure, ever.

Twenty years in, the process is calm on purpose. You get a real conversation first, a plan you understand second, and someone who has seen it before standing between you and every surprise.

Step 01

Consult

A straight conversation about where you are - buying, selling, PCS-ing, or just weighing options. English or Spanish. No pitch, no pressure, no cost.

Step 02

Plan

Betsy maps your budget, loan type, and must-haves into a realistic plan - what you qualify for, what to expect, and what the current Central Texas market is really doing.

Step 03

Execute

Showings, offers, builder contracts, inspections, and appraisals - handled by someone who has run them hundreds of times, so the small things do not become expensive ones.

Step 04

Close

Clear to close, keys in hand, and a Realtor you can still call after. Two decades of "call Betsy" referrals were built on exactly this last step.
